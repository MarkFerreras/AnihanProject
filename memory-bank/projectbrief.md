# Project Brief — Anihan SRMS

## Project Purpose
To develop a centralized Student Records Management System (SRMS) that helps Anihan Technical School improve the storage, retrieval, and integrity of student records, preventing the increasing challenges posed by the accumulation of physical records.

## Core Problem
Anihan Technical School faces operational inefficiencies and compliance risks due to over 40 years of accumulated physical records. The current system relies on a single Registrar manually managing scattered files (Google Forms, Excel sheets, handwritten index cards, physical green folders). This bottleneck delays daily operations and causes backlogs in Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) Special Orders (SO), currently delaying three batches (about 60 students). 

## Objectives
**General**: Address slow processing times by centralizing student records, relieving the overloaded duties of the registrar, and digitizing accumulated physical archives.

**Specific**:
- Design a centralized workflow for sorting, consolidating, and managing all required student records and TESDA Special Orders.
- Implement a secure digital information management system for creating, updating, retrieving, and retaining student records.
- Evaluate the system's usability, functionality, reliability, security, and efficiency based on ISO/IEC 25010 standards.
- Conduct a time and motion study comparing the current method to the provided SRMS.
- Evaluate KPIs: Special Order preparation time, processing efficiency, data accuracy, accessibility, and security.

## Scope and Limitations
Focuses on compiling and digitizing the existing records management and enrollment workflows within Anihan Technical School. Data is gathered directly from the current records and inter-related enrollment processes. It does not handle processes completely outside the creation and tracking of student records.

## Primary Beneficiaries
- **Registrar**: Heavily reduced workload by automating manual encoding, cross-checking, retrieval, and document preparation.
- **Students & Graduates**: Faster procurement of essential academic requirements (Special Orders, Transcript of Records, Certificates of Graduation) for employment and opportunities abroad.
- **Faculty & Staff**: Improved coordination, tracking, and academic monitoring.

## Stack Overview
- **Front-End**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES2024, Bootstrap 5.3, DataTables 2
- **Back-End**: Java 25, Spring Boot 4.0, Spring Security 7, Spring Validation, Spring Data JPA
- **Database**: MySQL 8 (Docker)
